About Us

This is a demo application developed by first-year CST students at BCIT (British Columbia Institute of Technology) as part of a school project. All posts, products, and content on this platform are demo data created for educational purposes only.

The app was designed to showcase a community cart system where users can collaborate on bulk shopping and benefit from group discounts. Please note that all content is fictional and not intended for real-world transactions.
